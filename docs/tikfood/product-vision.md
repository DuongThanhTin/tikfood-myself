# TikFood Product Vision

TikFood is realtime social food discovery: TikTok + Google Maps for food discovery.

The product helps people discover trending dishes and venues nearby through social proof, geo intelligence, and AI-generated summaries.

Core principles:

- Dish-first discovery
- Map-first discovery
- Social proof aggregation
- Realtime trend scoring
- AI-generated summaries
- Hidden gem detection
- Food graph, creator graph, trend graph, and geo intelligence
